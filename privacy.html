Privacy Policy

Privacy

Your data belongs to you. We don't sell it, share it, or monetize it.

Our Commitment

civ.works is built on a simple principle: you are not the product. Your civic participation should never be monetized, tracked, or sold to third parties.

What We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide you with our services:

  • Account information (email, username)
  • Content you create and share
  • Basic usage data to improve the platform

What We Don't Do

  • We don't sell your data
  • We don't share your data with advertisers
  • We don't track you across the web
  • We don't build behavioral profiles
  • We don't use your civic engagement to sell you products

How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used exclusively to:

  • Operate and maintain the platform
  • Provide you with the features you request
  • Improve the user experience
  • Protect the security and integrity of the platform
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Portability

Your data is yours. We're building on open protocols because we believe you should be able to take your content and connections with you if you choose to leave. That's not a bug—it's a feature.

Funding Model

civ.works is funded by subscriptions, not surveillance. When you pay for the service, we work for you—not for advertisers, not for data brokers, not for engagement metrics. This alignment is fundamental to our mission.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes via email or through the platform.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at [email protected].
